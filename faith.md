# Faith and Community

Faith and community have played an important role in my life, and I am interested in learning how people from different cultures express their beliefs and build supportive communities.

During my exchange, I hope to connect with local communities and learn how faith and shared values shape identity and communication across cultures.

## What I Will Explore
- Community gatherings
- Conversations about beliefs and culture
- How faith influences everyday life
- Building friendships through shared values
