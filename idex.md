# Faith, Football, and Friendship

## Connecting Across Cultures Through Study Abroad

Welcome to my creative project for my study abroad experience. This project explores how shared passions such as football, faith, and community create connections between people from different cultures.



## About the Project

Studying abroad offers the opportunity to step outside of familiar environments and experience new cultures and perspectives. Through this project, I will document how communication, sport, and shared values help build friendships and understanding across cultures.

Football has always been a way for me to connect with others regardless of background. During my exchange, I hope to join local teams and communities where sport becomes a common language that brings people together.

I will also reflect on how faith and community shape identity and communication across cultures.



## What I Will Share

Throughout my exchange, I will document:

- Experiences playing football and connecting with others through sport
- Cultural differences and learning moments
- Faith and community experiences abroad
- Reflections on meeting people from different backgrounds
- Stories of friendship and cultural exchange



## Goal of the Project

The goal of this project is to demonstrate how shared interests and values can build meaningful connections between people from different cultures. I hope this project encourages others to step outside their comfort zones and explore international experiences.
