# Football and Cultural Connection

Football is a universal language that connects people across cultures and backgrounds. One of my goals while studying abroad is to join a local football team or play recreationally with other students.

Through this experience, I hope to build friendships, learn about different cultural approaches to sport, and experience how football creates community regardless of nationality or language.

## What I Will Document
- Playing football abroad
- Meeting new teammates
- Cultural differences in sport
- Moments where sport helped build friendships
